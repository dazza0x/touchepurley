Our Story

We Didn't Come This Far to Stop Now

Five decades of passion, craft and community — right in the heart of Purley.

Est. 1975

Over 50 Years in Purley

Touché Hairdressing has been part of Purley's community since 1975 — and we've never stopped evolving.

We opened our doors when platform shoes were de rigueur and disco was king. Since then, we've styled our way through every era — the big perms of the 80s, the sleek bobs of the 90s, the ombre revolution of the 2010s, and now the expressive, individualised looks of today.

Through it all, one thing has remained constant: our commitment to exceptional hairdressing. Over those decades, we've built relationships not just with clients, but with entire families across Purley, Warlingham, Oxted and the wider Surrey area.

We believe in staying ahead of the curve. Our team regularly attends industry training, meaning you can always expect the latest techniques alongside timeless fundamentals.

"Hair is our passion — and every client who sits in our chair deserves to leave feeling their absolute best."
