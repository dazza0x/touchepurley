Organethic Pure Care

Ethical, sustainable hair care that works — because beauty shouldn't cost the planet.

9 April 2025 2 min read Products

There's a growing awareness in the beauty industry that the products we apply to hair and skin have implications far beyond the bathroom. At Touché Hairdressing, we take this seriously — which is why Organethic Pure Care (OPC) has become one of our most trusted ranges.

A Commitment to Natural Ingredients

OPC was founded on a straightforward principle: that professional hair care can be both highly effective and genuinely natural. Every formula in the range is built around plant-derived extracts and essential oils, selected both for their performance and their provenance.

The range is:

  • Free from sulfates
  • Free from parabens
  • Free from artificial colourants and synthetic fragrances
  • Formulated without silicones that coat the hair and build up over time

The result is hair care that genuinely nourishes rather than masking damage with synthetic coatings — and a cleaner, healthier scalp over time.

Ethical and Sustainable Practices

What distinguishes OPC beyond ingredient quality is its comprehensive commitment to ethical practice. This extends across the entire supply chain:

  • Fair labour standards — OPC works with suppliers who meet rigorous fair trade and labour standards throughout the ingredient sourcing process
  • Biodegradable packaging — The brand has committed to sustainable packaging across its product lines, minimising plastic waste
  • Supply chain transparency — Customers and salons can trace the origin of key ingredients, ensuring accountability at every stage

For a salon like Touché, whose clients increasingly care about where their products come from and what impact they have, this transparency is genuinely valuable.

Advanced Professional Formulation

OPC isn't simply a "natural" range that sacrifices performance for principles. Their formulations are developed with professional use in mind, offering targeted solutions across the full spectrum of hair needs:

  • Hydration — For dry, thirsty hair lacking moisture and elasticity
  • Repair — For chemically treated, heat-damaged or structurally compromised hair
  • Volume — For fine or limp hair needing body and lift without heaviness
  • Colour care — Formulations specifically designed to extend colour longevity and enhance vibrancy
  • Scalp therapy — Targeted treatments for sensitivity, imbalance or irritation

This versatility makes OPC a genuinely useful professional range — we can tailor treatments to individual clients rather than applying a single-formula approach.

Transparency as a Principle

One of the qualities we most respect about Organethic Pure Care is their commitment to education. Rather than hiding behind marketing language, the brand provides detailed ingredient breakdowns and offers training programmes for salon teams — so we can confidently explain exactly what we're applying to your hair and why.

When a client asks "what's in that?" — and increasingly they do — we can give a genuine, informed answer rather than a branded platitude.

Beauty with a Story

Every OPC product represents a process of mindful selection — from the botanical gardens where raw materials are sourced to the laboratory where they're refined into professional formulations. That story of care and intention is something we believe is worth sharing.

In a market flooded with products making extravagant claims, OPC stands out for saying what it does and doing what it says.

Ask About an OPC Treatment

Luxury hair treatments using Organethic Pure Care start from £20. Available as a standalone service or as an add-on to any colour or cutting appointment.
