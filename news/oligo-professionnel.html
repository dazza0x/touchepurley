Oligo Professionnel

Botanical performance meets salon-grade results — discover why we trust this range with your hair.

9 April 2025 1 min read Products

At Touché Hairdressing, we're selective about the products we use. Every range we introduce to the salon is chosen with care — for its performance, safety profile and the values behind it. Oligo Professionnel ticks every box.

High-Quality Ingredients, Thoughtfully Sourced

Oligo Professionnel's philosophy is rooted in the belief that professional hair care should never compromise on ingredient quality. Their formulations combine botanical extracts, essential oils and proteins — all working together to nourish hair at a structural level, not just coat the surface.

Key ingredients include:

  • Argan oil — Rich in vitamin E and fatty acids, argan oil moisturises deeply, reduces frizz and adds shine without heaviness
  • Aloe vera — Soothing and hydrating for both scalp and strand, aloe vera supports healthy hair growth and calms irritation
  • Sunflower seed extract — Lightweight and nourishing, providing UV protection and enhancing natural lustre
  • Essential oils — Carefully selected for scalp health and fragrance, with no synthetic masking agents

Free from Harsh Chemicals

Oligo Professionnel formulations are free from sulfates, parabens and other potentially harmful additives that strip hair of its natural oils and disrupt the scalp's microbiome. This is particularly important for colour-treated hair — a harsh shampoo or conditioner can fade colour rapidly and leave hair feeling brittle and dull.

By using products that are gentle on the hair's structure, we help our colour and treatment work last longer, look better and feel healthier.

Salon-Grade Performance

There's a meaningful difference between consumer products you find on supermarket shelves and professional-grade formulations developed for salon use. Oligo Professionnel sits firmly in the latter camp — concentrated, expertly balanced and designed for the demands of professional application.

Whether we're preparing your hair for a colour service, treating it post-chemical process, or simply giving it an intensive conditioning boost, Oligo Professionnel delivers consistent, measurable results.

Scalp Care as Well as Strand Care

Healthy hair starts at the scalp, and Oligo Professionnel's formulations reflect this. By supporting scalp health — maintaining moisture balance, soothing sensitivity and promoting circulation — the range helps create the ideal conditions for strong, healthy growth from root to tip.

Experience It for Yourself
